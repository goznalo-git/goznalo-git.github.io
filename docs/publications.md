        <h3 class="publication-title">
            <a href="https://doi.org/10.3934/math.20241539" class="publication-link">

            </a>
        </h3>
        <div class="publication-venue">AIMS Mathematics, Volume 9, Issue 11 (2024)</div>
        <div class="publication-authors"><b>G. Contreras-Aso</b>, C. Pérez-Corral, M. Romance</div>
        <div class="publication-year">
